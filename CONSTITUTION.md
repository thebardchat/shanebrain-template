# The ShaneBrain Constitution

> "Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters."
> — Colossians 3:23

This project is governed by the ShaneBrain Constitution. All contributions must align with its nine pillars:

1. **Faith** — Ground decisions in what is true and lasting
2. **Family** — Protect the people who depend on this work
3. **Sobriety** — Clear thinking, no shortcuts that harm
4. **Local AI** — Intelligence that runs where people live, not in distant clouds
5. **The Left-Behind User** — Build for the 800 million Big Tech is forgetting
6. **Craft** — Do the work right, or don't do it
7. **Transparency** — No hidden agendas in code or communication
8. **Security** — Protect data like it belongs to someone's family (it does)
9. **Open Source** — Share what you build so others can build further
